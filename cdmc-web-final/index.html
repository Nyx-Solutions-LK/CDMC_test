About Us

The Concept of Soul Center

Soul Centers are faith-based communities formally recognized under the Christian Leaders Alliance (CLA) in the United States of America. These centers operate under the leadership of credentialed officiants who are listed in the Christian Leaders Minister Directory and are entrusted with initiating, guiding, and overseeing ministry activities.

Soul Centers are not limited to a single model; rather, they are shaped by the unique gifts, training, and calling of each credentialed minister. This flexibility allows each center to effectively serve its community while remaining rooted in biblical principles and mission.

Introduction

The Christian Discipleship Mentor Center (CDMC), Sri Lanka

The Christian Discipleship Mentor Center (CDMC), based in Sri Lanka, is one such expression of a Soul Center. CDMC is dedicated to nurturing and developing Christian leaders through discipleship. Its mission includes proclaiming the Gospel, equipping believers, and mentoring disciples in alignment with the Great Commission of our Lord Jesus Christ.

Through its focused approach, CDMC seeks to empower individuals to grow spiritually, lead faithfully, and actively participate in advancing God’s Kingdom.

The Need for a Mentor Center in Sri Lanka

A leadership vacuum exists in the training and education of Christian Discipleship in Sri Lanka. Most prospective trainees are hesitant to follow Christian Discipleship courses due to financial limitations and their inability to attend classes physically. Therefore, online education remains an ideal solution for such individuals.

We also observed that individuals who have commenced online courses through self-studies have encountered various theoretical and practical issues. Therefore, mentoring remains a vital requirement. The establishment of a Mentor Center would enable us to provide the necessary support these students require while ensuring the continuity of their studies and nurturing them in their journey towards true Christian Discipleship.

Objectives

Christian Discipleship Mentor Center (CDMC, Colombo)

CDMC is a registered Soul Centre of the Christian Leaders Alliance, USA, and is located in Moratuwa, Sri Lanka. The CDMC has already commenced a Training of Trainers (TOT) program to meet the need for trainers/Mentors for future discipleship training programs planned by the center.

The prospective Mentors will undergo online training at the Christian Leaders Institute, USA. Upon successfully completing their education, the Mentors will, in turn, provide guidance to individuals undergoing Christian discipleship training in Sri Lanka.

01

To invite individuals who are keen on following Christian discipleship training.

02

To offer personalized guidance to prospective students and address any concerns or queries related to the discipleship curriculums of individual courses.

03

To empower and commission mentored students who have successfully completed Christian discipleship courses to become dedicated disciples of Jesus Christ.

How do we Serve?

Mentoring

Mentoring is an integral part of Christian discipleship training. At CDMC we provide guidance, support, and encouragement to individuals as they grow in their faith and seek to live out the teachings of Christ. We equip our mentees with practical skills and knowledge to live out their faith in their everyday lives, in their relationships, work, and community involvement. Join us to learn how we can help you discover the unique gifts and talents God has bestowed upon you.

Supporting

Supporting individuals in their Christian discipleship journey is another crucial aspect CDMC focuses on. We do this through our small group discussions known as discipleship groups or cell groups. Small group communities foster a sense of belonging and encourage mutual accountability and encouragement among members. We also provide pastoral care and intercessory support.

Enabling

Enabling is another vital aspect of Christian discipleship training at CDMC. Serving is a transformative journey that empowers individuals to embrace their God-given potential. At CDMC we provide individuals with the resources and skills. This enables them to actively engage in fulfilling their calling as followers of Christ and enables them to live out their faith authentically and make a positive impact in their communities.

Directing

Directing plays another crucial role in Christian discipleship training. This involves providing guidance, leadership, and direction to disciples to navigate their journey of faith and service. CDMC is committed to helping individuals discover God's plan for their lives. This involves helping them make intentional choices aligned with Biblical principles and guiding them towards a deeper relationship with Christ.

About Me
